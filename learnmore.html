About NII

The Neuralink Institute of Innovation (NII) is a premier engineering and technology institution dedicated to pioneering advancements in artificial intelligence, neuroengineering, robotics, and futuristic computing.

Key Features

  • Advanced Research Labs in AI, BCI, and Quantum Computing
  • Industry Collaboration with leading tech firms
  • Innovation Incubators for student startups
  • Smart Campus with AI-powered assistants

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech/M.Tech in Neuroengineering & Brain-Computer Interfaces
  • B.Tech in AI & Robotics
  • B.Tech in Cybersecurity & Quantum Computing
  • M.Tech in Human-Computer Interaction
  • Ph.D. in Computational Neuroscience & Artificial Intelligence

Mission & Vision

Mission: To revolutionize human potential by integrating neuroscience, AI, and next-gen computing.

Vision: To be the world’s leading neurotechnology-driven institute.
